▶ Why did my credits disappear?

Credits are deducted each time the bot successfully claims a shift for you. You can track your remaining balance in the popup and top-up through the dashboard.

▶ Why am I suddenly logged out?

If your subscription’s expiry date has passed, the extension force-logs you out and blocks new runs. Contact us to renew access.

▶ What PC and internet specs do I need?

Recommended for best performance
• Quad-core CPU (Intel i5 / Ryzen 5) (Windows)
• 8 GB RAM or more
• Strong Wi-Fi

Internet connection
• Stable broadband at ≥ 300 Mbps
• Avoid VPNs if you are encountering issues.
